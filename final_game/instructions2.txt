Slime Jump 
Goal of the Game:
Get as high as you can using the platforms 
 
Rules:
Use the spacebar to jump once and press the spacebar 
twice to double jump 
you die once you fall off the screen 
 
*Use return button or X to return to Menu 
*Scoreboard 1 => Wordle Scoreboard 
*Scoreboard 2 => Slime Jump Scoreboard
