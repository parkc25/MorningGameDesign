Goal of the Game: 
Guess the word before or on your sixth try 

Rules: 
Guess a five letter word 
If green that letter is in the correct spot and is a letter in the word 
If yellow that letter is in the word but not in the correct spot 
Press space to move to your next guess and enter to start over 
 
*Use return button or X to return to Menu 
*Scoreboard 1 => Wordle Scoreboard
